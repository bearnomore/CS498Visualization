The Age of the Countries

When talking about the age of a country, most of time we are thinking about the historical age of the country. Therefore, the 244-year old United States is a young kid to countries with thousands of years of history. However, countries are made and their physical ages can be represented by the median age of their own people.
Check out the physical age of countries by hovering your mouse on the map and you will see the median age of the population underneath the map.
